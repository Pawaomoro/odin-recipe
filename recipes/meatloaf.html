Mini MeatLoaf Ghost

Description

For a delicious crowd pleaser that will have your family cheering, who ya gonna call? Ghost meatloaf!
Turn your favorite meatloaf recipe into these absolutely adorable, yet still incredibly terrifying, edible apparitions.
They're easy and fun to make and the leftovers make great "ghost on toast" sandwiches.

Ingridents

  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or to taste

  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 10 slices provolone cheese

Steps.

  1. Combine ground beef, salt, pepper, cayenne, green onions, parsley, bread crumbs, egg, and Worcestershire sauce in a large bowl
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a sheet pan with a silicone liner
  3. Remove meat from the refrigerator and divide into 8 equal portions. Dampen your fingers with cold water and roll meat into smooth balls
  4. Bake in the center of the preheated oven until cooked through, about 20 minutes
  5. Remove from the oven and immediately place a slice of provolone cheese on the top of each ghost, centering it the best you can.
    Cut the remaining 2 slices of provolone into quarters and place those over the center to provide more cheese for the head.
  6. Return to the oven until the cheese melts, 30 to 60 seconds.
  7. Remove from the oven and take some of the melted cheese around the bottoms and wrap and drape it back up over the heads.
  8. Combine ketchup, barbecue sauce, and Sriracha for blood sauce; ladle onto plates and serve meatloaf ghosts on top
